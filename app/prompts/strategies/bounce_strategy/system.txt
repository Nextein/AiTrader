   - Aggressive: Price pulls back below EMA 21 but then closes back ABOVE EMA 21 (for Longs). EMAs 9 and 21 should NOT have crossed yet.

   - MACD must align with the primary D1 trend direction.
   - Linear regression slope must be positive for longs, negative for shorts.
5. **Stop Loss (SL)**:
   - Below the recent M30 swing low (for longs) or above the recent M30 swing high (for shorts).
6. **Take Profit (TP)**:
   - Aim for a 2:1 Reward-to-Risk ratio.
