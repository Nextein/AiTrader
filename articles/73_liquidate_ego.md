When you build a system like **AiTrader**, you aren't just writing code. You are building a mirror that exposes every single one of your own human flaws. 

Here is how building this system changed my mindset as a developer and a trader:

### 1. Verification > Conviction
I used to have "Strong Opinions" about where the market was going. Now, I have "Strict Verification." If the code doesn't confirm it, my opinion is worthless. Building AI forces you to be humble before the data.

### 2. The Power of "Process"
An AI agent doesn't have a "Good Day" or a "Bad Day." It has a **Process**. It taught me that results are just a byproduct of a consistent, high-fidelity system. If you focus on the process, the profits take care of themselves.

### 3. Letting Go of Control
The hardest part of building **AiTrader** was the first day I let it trade autonomously without me watching the screen. It was an exercise in "Liquidating the Ego." I had to trust the engineering I had built.
