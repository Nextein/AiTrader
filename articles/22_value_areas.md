1. **Point of Control (POC)**: The price level where the absolute most volume was traded. This is the "Fair Value." Price acts like a magnet to the POC.
2. **Value Area High (VAH)**: The upper bound of the range where 70% of the volume occurred.
3. **Value Area Low (VAL)**: The lower bound of that 70% range.
