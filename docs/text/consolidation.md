# Consolidation

## Utility

Period of market cooling off. Sideways amrket movement.

## Implementation

Consolidation above support is bearish.

Consolidation below resistance is considered bullish.

Consolidation in a larger sideways range is neutral.

Use fibonaccit to predict consolidation if it is occurring an impulse wave (1,3,5).

Order Flow CVD DIvergences are useful during consolidation to see where limit orders are absorbing and in which direction.

Trading consolidation is simple: Go long at the range low or short at the range high (SFP or FA) of the outer boundaries are most effective.

We can trade the breakout when combined with strong order flow. He doesn't use this strategy.

He prefers trading in anticipation of the breakout.

## Tips

Consolidation below resistance can offer great TT on short entries. Generally considered bullish, but it can offer a great RR on short trade.

Nothing is ever black and white, market context will always be.

Look at order flow and volume on breakouts.
