# Chart Champions Education

## Risk Management
### Description

It is not as simple as choosing Bitcoin or a stock to trade, entering the position and making a profit.

This risk management module will teach you how to calculate your position size in accordance with managing the risk of your overall capital.

For example, when you open a trade that results in profit, your overall balance is increased. Conversely, if you open a trade that results in a loss, your overall balance will decrease.

 

Learn how to calculate your exact risk in regards to your position size, and you will never suffer unnecessary losses.

### Utility

Risk managemetn is the most important part of trading because it makes you avoid huge losses on a losing trade. Probabilities, you will take a loss, you need to be able to withstand losing trades. 100x leverage you will blopw up.

Losses are part of trading and risk management is a key.

### Implementation

Work out entry price and trigger, invalidation and stop loss (at invalidation) before taking a trade.

Have a strategy for position sizing so you don't enter random amounts on every trade.

Don't risk more than 1% of capital on any trade.

Increase risk with confidence and experience.
EXTRA: He can ramp up to max 5% in a trade if the market is hot.
He would never go above 5% risk per trade.

### Tips

Always set hard SL.

Don't get greedy by not taking profits.
If you've planned the trade and have a TP1, make sure you take it regardless of what you think.

Always plant the trade, trade the plan, then journal the trade.
We're not entering emotionally, we wait patiently, wait for the trade to come to our planned entry, then we take the trade confidently and journal the trade to improvde ourselves.

Warning: Trading on high leverage is extremely risky and is not recommended.

 

For the experienced trader, leverage can provide a benefit to a high probability position. However, the experienced trader is aware that there are both pros and cons to using leverage and margin.

Many professional traders will disclose that they do not use leverage, nor do they find it necessary. At Chart Champions we will never advocate high-risk leverage trading, yet it is still important for you to be educated on various trading instruments and the risks they pose.

Remember, protecting your capital is your priority.

### Utility

A) Trade more contracts than you have for larger position sizes and account growth quicker via bigger profits.

B) Less exposure on exchange when used correctly. This is best use case of leverage.

Larger losses.
More leverage = closer liquidation price.

Liquidation = potentisl of losing sll your account in 1 trade. (Cross leverage)

### Implementation

1x leverage:
$1000 position size. +- $10 for every 1% move.

100x leverage:
$100,000 position size. +-$1000 for every 1% move.

### Implementation

Cross vs isolated margin.

Don't use more than 5x leverage. It's very dangerous.


### tips

Using cross will keep liquidation far away.

Isolated will control max loss in the event of liquidation.

Always place a SL at invalidation price. Don't rely on liquidation as a SL placement.
